Welcome to the Animal Welfare Society Port Elizabeth

The Animal Welfare Society (AWS) of Port Elizabeth is a safe shelter for animals that have been lost, neglected and abandoned.

All of the animals that we house, are up for adoption to the public.

Not only do we look after our animals, but we also run a service whereby we will respond to any reports of animal cruelty.

We have a very helpful team of 40 that make up our staff. We have different sections to our staff, such as those who look after the animals, veterinarians and those who respond to cases of abuse.

We accept any form of donation from the public, weather it be in the form of food, beds, blankets or if you are kind enough to spare some money for our organization, we would accept it with open arms. All donations are spent on the animals and the maintenance and upgrade of the standard of hygiene within the living area for the animals.

In the video above, you will evidently see that we offer help to community, voluntarily. At the AWS we are happy to offer a helping hand, when needed.
